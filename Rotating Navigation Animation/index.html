If My Dog Could Talk Personalized Story Book

If your dog could talk, imagine the things you’d learn! This book celebrates your beloved family pet, and explains—from a canine perspective—all the funny things he does. The dog’s name appears on the book cover and throughout the book (there’s also space for family members’ names), and you can personalize the illustration to create a dog that looks just like your own. A personal message from you printed at the front and back of the book makes it extra giftable for kids and adults alike. This 8x8 hardcover story book is intended for children of all ages.
